# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Author
Arna Adhikari

## Overview
This project analyzes how users move through a marketing funnel, highlights drop-off points, and provides practical, data-driven strategies to improve conversions and revenue.

## Objectives
- Calculate traffic-to-lead conversion rate
- Calculate lead-to-customer conversion rate
- Identify funnel drop-off points
- Compare performance across marketing channels
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## Dataset
Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset (UCI)

Contains:
- Customer contact details
- Marketing channels
- Campaign interactions
- Previous outcomes
- Subscription result (conversion)

Records: 45,000+ customers  
Target Variable: `y` (Subscribed: Yes/No)

## Key Insights
- Significant drop-off between lead and customer stage
- Cellular channel shows highest conversions
- Excessive campaign contacts reduce success rate
- Targeted follow-ups improve outcomes

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing channels (cellular/phone)
- Limit repeated campaign calls
- Optimize lead nurturing strategies
- Prioritize high-intent customer segments
