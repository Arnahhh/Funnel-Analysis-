# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Author
Arna Adhikari

## Overview
The project studies user movement through the marketing funnel while identifying points of user abandonment and delivering evidence-based solutions which help businesses achieve better conversion rates and increased revenue.

## Objective

The research investigates user behavior through marketing funnels while identifying points of user exit. The study provides data-backed solutions which help improve customer acquisition and revenue growth as its primary aim.

## Key Questions Addressed

The team will determine how much website visitors turn into leads.
The team will determine how many leads become paying customers.
The team will determine which stages of the funnel experience customer drop-off.
The team will assess how different marketing channels perform in their delivery of results.
The team will assess how successful different marketing campaigns performed.
The team will provide specific recommendations which businesses can implement to achieve better outcomes.

## Dataset Description

Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset contains customer contact details and marketing channels and campaign interactions and previous outcomes and subscription result (conversion) records of 45000 customers. The dataset contains complete information about customer contact details and marketing channels and campaign interactions and previous outcomes and subscription result (conversion).

## Tools and Technologies Used

The system uses Python and Pandas and NumPy and Matplotlib and Seaborn and Jupyter Notebook to create its output.

## Data Preparation

The team removed all duplicate records during data preparation while they fixed all missing data problems. The team converted conversion target data from Yes/No to 1/0 format.

## Methodology

The team recorded all users who reached out to them and counted all leads they produced and all customers who bought their products.

Performance Analysis

The performance analysis includes assessment of how well conversion rates differ between different channels and how well campaigns perform and how drop-off points affect results and how two variables show relationship between each other.

## Visualization and Presentation

The presentation shows information through funnel charts and bar plots and conversion rate comparisons and campaign trends.

## Key Findings

The business research found that customers who become leads experience a major drop between their current status and reaching the next customer status. The cellular channel shows highest conversions of customers. Excessive campaign contacts reduce success rate. Targeted follow-ups improve outcomes.

## Recommendations

The company should focus its efforts on marketing channels which have shown strong performance through their cellular and phone channels. The company should reduce its marketing campaigns through multiple calls to customers. The company needs to improve its approaches for lead nurturing. The company should focus its attention on customers who show strong intent to buy its products.
